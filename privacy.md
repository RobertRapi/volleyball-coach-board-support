# Privacy Policy – Volleyball Coach Board

Last updated: 2026

Volleyball Coach Board does not collect, store, or share personal information.

## Data Collection

The app does not collect personal data, usage analytics, location data, or contact information.

## Data Storage

All drawings and diagrams created in the app are stored locally on the user’s device.
The app does not upload or transmit this data to external servers.

## Third-Party Services

The app does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Children’s Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated if app features change. Any updates will be posted on this page.
