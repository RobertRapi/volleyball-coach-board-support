## About the App
Volleyball Coach Board is a digital whiteboard for volleyball coaches.  
It allows coaches to draw tactics on a volleyball court during training sessions and matches using a phone or tablet.

## FAQ

**How do I draw on the court?**  
Use your finger. Select colour, brush size and line style from the menu.

**How do I change the court background?**  
Open the menu and choose another court template.

**How do I undo a mistake?**  
Use the Undo button. Redo restores the last action.

**How do I save a diagram?**  
Use Save. The diagram is stored as an image on your device.

**What is the BENCH feature?**  
The BENCH lets you store player names and jersey numbers and place them on the court.
